## Price Parity Requirements

### Agency Model (Apple, Most eBooks)
- Publisher sets price
- Retailer takes 30%
- Price parity clauses may apply

### Wholesale Model (Amazon Print)
- Publisher sets list price
- Retailer can discount freely
- Royalty based on list price

### Matching Requirements
| Platform | Requires Price Match? | Notes |
