## About the Series

**101 Ways to Die in a Hostel** is a 103-book horror-comedy series following travelers who meet gruesome ends in hostels around the world. Each standalone novel combines dark humor with genuine horror, satirizing travel culture while delivering suspenseful, character-driven stories.

**Categories:**
- Books 1-20: Environmental Hazards
- Books 21-40: Fellow Travelers
- Books 41-60: Self-Inflicted
- Books 61-80: Supernatural/Bizarre
- Books 81-102: Circumstantial/Random
- Book 103: Series Finale
